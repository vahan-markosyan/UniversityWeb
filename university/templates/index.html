About Us

A major element that distinguishes UFAR is the connection with Europe and the world that the university creates for its students. By obtaining a double diploma - French (European) and Armenian, acquiring international knowledge, and being in frequent contact with foreign professors, UFAR students benefit from strong international ties and enter the European reality.

International Development: This comes from the ties that UFAR has with various structures uniting Francophone countries, such as the OIF (International Organization of Francophonie), the AUF (University Agency of Francophonie), territorial communities, Francophone agencies, etc. The strong relationships between UFAR and its partner universities reinforce the role of the UFAR as a true gateway to the world.

Language Excellence: This distinctive element is also based on the teaching of foreign languages, such as French and English. The UFAR pays special attention to the development of Francophonie among its students and in the country in general.
